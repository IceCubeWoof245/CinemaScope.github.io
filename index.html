About Us

Our company, CinemaScope, has been established for approximately 5 years. We currently have 5 cinemas across the country, including in London, Birmingham and Leeds. We show a variety of films in our cinemas, most of which are brand new. As well as this, we sometimes run film festivals which show a variety of older and contemporary movies for our customers to enjoy.

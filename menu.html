Our Menu

Our cinemas all offer a wide variety of snacks and drinks that you can enjoy while you watch your favourite movies!

For information on allergens please ask a member of staff in the cinema.


Popcorn

Our cinemas have salted popcorn, sweet popcorn and a new toffee flavoured popcorn.


Snacks

We have a large variety of sncack for you to bring to your favourite movies. We have a variety of Cadbury chocolate including dairy milk, caramel and fruit and nut bars. We also have a variety of Haribo sweets for the little ones to enjoy.


Drinks

Our cinemas offer many chilled fizzy drinks, including Pepsi, Sprite and Fanta. We also have a colourful selection of slush puppies to enjoy during the movie.
